About Me

I keep everything
simple and working.

I'm Jessrell M. Custodio, a BSIT student at Mindoro State University with a strong focus on frontend development and web design. My interest lies at the intersection of entrepreneurship and technology — I build websites that help businesses establish their presence and thrive in the digital era.

I don't believe in overcomplicating things. A business doesn't need a bloated system to succeed online — it needs a website that loads fast, communicates clearly, and turns visitors into customers. That's what I deliver: practical, results-focused web solutions built with clean code.

Projects I've built:

  • A fully functional e-commerce web application
  • Landing pages and business portfolio sites
  • A digital e-library platform
  • A real-time messaging web application
  • A fitness tracking web app
